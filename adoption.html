Akamaru

Akamaru (赤丸) is a ninja dog from Konohagakure's Inuzuka Clan. Akamaru has been described as active and devoted. This can be seen from his interactions with people. He is also a member of Team Kurenai.

Happy

Happy (ハッピー) is an Exceed (Cat with Wings) from Extalia, and a member of the Fairy Tail Guild. Happy is very loyal. He loves fish more than anything else, and he doesn't like dogs.

Chopper

Tony Tony Chopper (トニートニー・チョッパー) is the doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. Chopper is a reindeer that ate the Hito Hito no Mi, a Devil Fruit that allows him to transform into a human. He loves cotton candy!

Kurama

Kurama (九喇嘛), more commonly known as the Nine-Tails (九尾, Kyūbi), was one of the nine tailed beasts. Kurama is a snarky, red-orange coloured fox with black fur around its red eyes, and nine long tails.


Ein

Ein (アイン Ain) is a Pembroke Welsh Corgi with greatly enhanced intelligence. Ein became part of the Bebop crew and was a good friend of Edward.


Hamtaro

Hamtaro (ハム太郎) is a male hamster. Hamtaro is always there for his friends, is very selfless and kindhearted towards everyone he meets and loves to have fun.


Osushi

Osushi (オスシ, Osushi) in his dog form, is a small white Pomeranian. In his Dark Dragon form, Osushi is a large, black dragon with a light underbelly.


Kirara

Kirara (雲母きらら) is an adorable adult-sized feline, but with the body proportions of a kitten.. She has red eyes, a diamond-shaped marking on her forehead, and two fluffy tails.
