Meet the Owner

Jet Black

Jet has always been an animal lover. As a young child, he began collecting all manner of "pets", including snakes, turtles, guinea pigs, ducks, rabbits, horses, kittens, and of course, dogs. As an adult, Jet's love of animals has only grown. That love, coupled with his savvy business skills, background in psychology, and an honest desire to help people, assures that he and the puppy shop is a match made in heaven.
